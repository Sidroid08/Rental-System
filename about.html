Who We Are?

At Fashion Rental, we're a team of passionate third-year IT students from A.C. Patil College of Engineering, Kharghar. Our mission is to make fashion accessible and sustainable for everyone. As young innovators, we're committed to providing a seamless platform where you can rent stunning outfits for any occasion, all while promoting the importance of eco-friendly fashion choices. We're here to redefine how you experience fashion—one rental at a time!

What We Offer?

We offer a curated selection of stylish, high-quality outfits perfect for every occasion—be it weddings, parties, or corporate events. Our easy-to-use platform allows you to browse, select, and rent outfits with just a few clicks. Enjoy the convenience of doorstep delivery, flexible rental periods, and hassle-free returns.

Why Choose Us?

Choosing Fashion Rental means choosing convenience, affordability, and sustainability. We believe in making luxury fashion accessible without the commitment of ownership. With our diverse collection and excellent customer service, renting your dream outfit has never been easier or more enjoyable.
